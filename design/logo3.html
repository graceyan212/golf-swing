Swing Check — motion & analysis marks
These read as your swing being tracked and measured, not just "golf." The app finds your path, positions and plane — so the mark shows exactly that.

Four marks

A · Swing plane
Your plane (pine) vs. the ideal (dashed) — exactly what the app measures.
B · Tracked path
The swing traced through address, top and impact — the positions it grades.
C · The golfer
A player at the top of the backswing. The most literal "swing."
D · Ball in motion
A struck ball tracing its path — marries object with movement.
