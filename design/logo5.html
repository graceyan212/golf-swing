Swing Check — bounce, refined
Thinner stroke (~½ the weight), the ball much bigger relative to it, and a real bounce arc: drop → contact → launch up to the ball. Four curve treatments.

Four curves

1 · Single bounce
One clean bounce up to the ball. Thin stroke, big ball.
2 · Steeper launch
Sharper rise off the bounce — more energy.
3 · Double bounce
Two bounces then launch — most literally "bouncing."
4 · Brass ball
Same bounce, solid brass ball. Warmest, simplest.
